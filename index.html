About Charlotte

My Journey to Terra Mater

As a certified postpartum coach, I understand the profound transformation that motherhood brings. My own journey opened my eyes to the need for gentle, embodied support during this sacred transition. I believe that every mother deserves to feel held, understood, and empowered as she navigates the beautiful complexity of raising children while honoring her own needs and growth.

Our Coaching Philosophy

At Terra Mater Coaching, we embrace a philosophy rooted in somatic support and embodied empowerment. Our approach combines gentle presence with practical wisdom, helping mothers connect with their innate strength while providing the deep care needed during this transformative time. We honor the "Mother Earth" within every woman—that deep, nurturing wisdom that deserves to be cared for in return.
